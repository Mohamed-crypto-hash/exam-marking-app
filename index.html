Exam Marking App

Overview

The **Exam Marking App** automates the process of grading exams, leveraging **AI** and **OCR** technology. Teachers can upload scanned or photographed exam papers, which are processed and graded instantly. The app provides detailed feedback, performance reports, and ensures efficient, accurate grading.

Description

The app uses Optical Character Recognition (OCR) to extract text from scanned or photographed exam papers, making it compatible with handwritten and printed content. After extracting the answers, **AI algorithms** automatically mark them based on a customizable marking scheme. Teachers can review results, provide instant feedback, and track student performance through detailed reports. This app reduces grading time, ensures accuracy, and helps educators focus on teaching.
